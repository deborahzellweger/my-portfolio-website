adibilis

the calm after the storm

Brand concept and design for a software & website development startup in Basel, Switzerland.

Adibilis' main focus is to bring calm and structure into old and redundant processes by understanding the customer needs and developing bespoke solutions therefor.

We've embraced that concept and created a brand identity that draws it's inspiration from a lighthouse in a storm, offering guidance and clarity when everything around it is chaos.

Designing a logo mark that's been derived from morse code ties in to the established concept, whilst interpreting code in a different way. Paired with a confident but welcoming logo type it transmits the brand values of empathy, reliability and authenticity.

The colour scheme's been selected to match the overall vibe and provide a means to create further digital collateral that can be designed for both daytime and nighttime mode.
