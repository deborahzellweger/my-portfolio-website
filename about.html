My obsession with design started in school. Unknowingly. Notes in workbooks had to be well structured. Colour coded. With impeccable handwriting. Basically ready for print. 100’000 copies at least! Had I known then that design was an actual career one could pursue - I would have been laughing!

Instead, I was completely clueless how to spend half a century in business and followed the most given advice in Switzerland to someone like me: an admin apprenticeship at an office.

My creative-outlet for the years to come turned out to be MsOffice - inspiring, I know. But word within my team began to spread quickly - no pun intended - and I soon became the go-to-wizard of spreadsheets, word docs and presentation slides.

It only dawned on me after relocating to London that my wizardry could help people outside of my team as well! The seed of career-change was planted and I watered it every Sunday. For 9-months. At Shillington College for Graphic Design. MsOffice was just the beginning.

What gets me out of bed today is my joy and drive to help young entrepreneurs kick-start their business ventures with a bang! The brand and web designs we create set solid foundations that look and feel professional, whilst always adding a fair bit of fun and lightness to reflect each unique vision, setting them apart from their competition.
