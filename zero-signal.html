ZERO SIGNAL

let the bubbles comfort you

ZERO SIGNAL’s mission is to educate people on the signs of burning out in order to prevent it and teach them coping mechanisms for a more balanced life.

Naming the event, in essence, was naming one of the main culprits of burning out: always being online.

The bubbles where drawn from familiar shapes we find on our mobile devises. The identity aims at counteracting everything related to stress with shapes, forms, colours and TOV that evoke a feeling of fuzz and comfort.

Huge ‘signal-cancelling’ comfort bubbles are planted all over the city, grabbing the stressed workforces’ attention and inviting them inside to de-compress and breathe.

Inside the bubbles, visitors find a relaxing, no signal space where different activities are taking place: from coping mechanism like breathing techniques to physical activities like yoga.
