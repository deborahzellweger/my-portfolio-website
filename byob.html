BYOB

brew your own beer - app design

User experience and interface design for a mobile application intended to support people on their journey to brew their own beer: BYOB.

Inspired by nature, the colour scheme and iconery are unintrusive and really support the user journey to perfectly brewed beer.

Colour pop's of deep orange tell the brewer exactly where they are and what steps to take next.
