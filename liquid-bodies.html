LIQUID BODIES

formed for life

Cem’s movement training in Zurich combines strength, balance and mobility and mash's it up for an all-round training - very choreo style.

We’ve embraced that lightness and fun to design a identity full of life. The brand elements developed on the one hand underline the training and movement itself.

The typeface on the other hand allows the client to simply create their own assets for web and social media, retaining that flexible expression.
