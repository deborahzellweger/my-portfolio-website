LIQUID BODIES

formed for life

Cem's approach to physical fitness is truly unique and comprehensive. Drawing inspiration from the Ido Portal Ideology and guided by a structured methodology, his training places a strong emphasis on getting your body moving and formed for life.

The goal for this design was to seamlessly embody Liquid Bodies' core values within the logo: open, light and with a strong sense of community.

Given Cem's structured training approach, we aimed for a design that reflects his methodical approach while maintaining a sense of playfulness and flexibility.

The resulting logo was a perfect match. The stretched letters could even be repurposed, transforming them into dynamic forms - little icons that symbolize the various facets of Cem's training methodology.

To extend the friendly yet robust brand identity we've drawn inspiration from the diverse terrains that Liquid Bodies utilizes in their training and carefully curated five distinctive brand colors.
